Course Description:

This course has been designed to introduce the students to the various topics in Deep Learning which has taken the world by storm in the form of various applications including ChatGPT, Atlas, etc.The students will be able to apply deep learning to a variety of tasks that are pertinent to various engineering as well as societal problems.We will learn about the basics of deep neural nets and also cover specialised topics like Language based models, Graph Nets along with hands-on sessions.


Prerequisites:

  • Open to all Backgrounds 📪
  • High-School Mathematical Concepts (Linear Algebra, Calculus) 🔢
  • Basic Python Skills 🐍
  • Tons of Curiosity ⚡

Topics to be Covered:

  • Introduction to Deep Learning and Current State of Art
  • Neural Networks & Backpropagation
  • Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN)
  • Optimization
  • Natural Language Processing
  • Language Based Models
  • Generative Models
